Who is Emma Stone?

Emma Stone is an American actress. She was born on November 6, 1988, in Scottsdale, Arizona. Stone began acting as a child and rose to prominence in the late 2000s with roles in films like “Superbad” and “Zombieland.”
She has since appeared in numerous successful films, including “Easy A,” “The Help,” “Birdman,” “La La Land,” and “The Favourite.” Stone has won several awards for her performances, including an Academy Award, a BAFTA Award, and a Golden Globe Award.

Emma Stone Easy A

“Easy A” is a 2010 teen comedy film directed by Will Gluck and starring Emma Stone. The movie follows high school student Olive Penderghast, who lies about losing her virginity and then finds herself at the center of a rumor mill. The film received positive reviews for its sharp writing and Emma Stone’s performance.

Emma Stone La La Land

“La La Land” is a 2016 American romantic comedy-drama musical film directed by Damien Chazelle, and starring Emma Stone and Ryan Gosling. The movie tells the story of a struggling actress and a jazz pianist who fall in love while trying to make it in Los Angeles. “La La Land” received critical acclaim and was a box office success, grossing over $446 million worldwide. Emma Stone won the Academy Award for Best Actress for her role in the film.
