Denzel Washington Fences

“Fences” is a 2016 American drama film directed by Denzel Washington, based on the Pulitzer Prize-winning play of the same name by August Wilson. The film stars Denzel Washington, Viola Davis, and Stephen Henderson, all of whom reprised their roles from the 2010 Broadway revival of the play.
The story follows Troy Maxson (Washington), a former baseball player who works as a garbage collector in 1950s Pittsburgh and struggles with his family relationships, race relations, and personal regrets.
