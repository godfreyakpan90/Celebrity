Jennifer Lawrence plays the lead role of Dominika Egorova, with supporting performances from Joel Edgerton, Matthias Schoenaerts, Charlotte Rampling, Mary-Louise Parker, and Jeremy Irons. The film received mixed reviews from critics but was praised for Jennifer Lawrence’s performance.
